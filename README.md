# ☀️ Solar Energy Market Place

A decentralized blockchain-based marketplace for trading solar energy using smart contracts.

## 🚀 Overview

Solar Energy Market Place is a Web3 application that enables peer-to-peer buying and selling of solar energy credits using Ethereum smart contracts. The platform ensures transparency, automation, and trustless transactions through blockchain technology.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Traditional energy marketplaces:
- Lack transparency
- Require centralized intermediaries
- Have delayed settlements
- Involve high operational costs

This project eliminates middlemen by leveraging smart contracts.
